# WhatsApp notifications

WhatsApp is a major part of the storefront fulfillment experience. It is used for first-order updates, order-state messaging, and later linking eligible guest orders into a signed-in customer account.

## What this flow covers

- The first WhatsApp update after checkout
- The success-page message preview
- What happens when the customer signs in later
- Linking guest orders by WhatsApp verification
- Order-history prompts for customers who do not yet have a saved WhatsApp number

## What the customer should expect

- The original order still succeeds even if the customer skips account linking.
- Linking is about future convenience and order history, not making the current order valid.
- Using the same WhatsApp number across guest checkout and account settings makes order history much easier to connect.
