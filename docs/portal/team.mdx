# Team

Open the Team page at `https://clickcollect.coffee/portal/staff/` to manage access and location assignment.

The current portal uses **people with roles**, not named team groups. If you are looking for “teams”, use the Team page to add each person and assign them to the right location or locations.

## Verification and sign-in rules

- Every owner and team member must verify their email before the portal allows access.
- Invited team members receive a verification email first and only become fully usable in the Team UI after they open the link.
- The Team page now shows `Verified` or `Pending verification` for each person.
- If a person wants to use Google sign-in later, invite them with that same Gmail address from the start.
- A verified Gmail-based account can use Google sign-in going forward and does not need to rely on the password day to day.

## How roles work

| Role | What the role can do |
| --- | --- |
| Owner | Full account access, including owner management |
| Admin | Full portal access |
| Manager | Orders plus categories, drinks, and extras |
| Staff | Orders only |

Owners and admins always have all-location visibility. Managers and staff can be assigned to specific locations.

The form includes:

## Assign a team member to a location

### 4. Use **Assigned locations** for managers and staff

The `Assigned locations` multi-select lets you attach a person to one or more branches.

- Leave it blank only if the page allows the person to work across all locations.
- Owners and admins are treated as all-location users automatically.

<ScreenshotPlaceholder
  title="Assigned locations selector"
  pathHint="static/img/portal/team/04-assigned-locations.png"
/>

### 5. Save and verify the list view

After saving, confirm the person now shows the correct:

- Role badge
- Assigned locations
- Active badge
- Verification badge

<ScreenshotPlaceholder

### 6. Resend verification when someone misses the first email

If the owner or a pending team member did not receive the original verification message, use the visible **Resend verification** action from the Team page.

- The owner row exposes a resend action while the owner email is still pending.
- Each pending team member row also exposes a resend action.
- Once the person verifies successfully, the resend action disappears and the badge changes to `Verified`.

## Billing and ownership notes

### 7. Watch for team-member billing prompts

Adding an active non-owner can trigger billing if the account has no open team-member capacity left for the current cycle.

### 8. Handle owner changes carefully

Only the current owner can add, edit, or remove owner-level access.

## Good practice

- Give managers the minimum access they need instead of defaulting to admin.
- Keep inactive people on inactive status instead of deleting them immediately if you still need their audit trail.
- Recheck location assignment every time a branch opens, closes, or changes hours.
